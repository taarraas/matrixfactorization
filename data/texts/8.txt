Opposition claim protestor found dead in Bahrain
21/04/2012 - 10:55:01

Bahrain's opposition claim a protester has been found dead after a night of clashes with police.

Security has been tightened on the streets of Bahrain's capital Manama after clashes ahead of tomorrow's Grand Prix.

Pro-democracy campaigners are set to intensify demonstrations there, but race organisers and the kingdom's rulers insist it is right the race should go ahead.
